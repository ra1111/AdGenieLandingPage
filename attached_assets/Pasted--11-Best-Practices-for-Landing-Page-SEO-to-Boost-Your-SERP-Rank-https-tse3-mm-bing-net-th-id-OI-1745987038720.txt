## üìç Keyword Placement Strategy

**1. Page Title (Meta Title):**
- Include primary keywords within the first 60 characters.
- Example: "Automated Ad Generator for Shopify | Create Social Media Ads Easily" ([Adding keywords for SEO to your Shopify store](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/promoting-marketing/seo/adding-keywords?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

**2. Meta Description:**
- Craft a compelling description incorporating primary and secondary keywords.
- Keep it under 160 characters.
- Example: "Boost your Shopify sales with our automated ad creation app. Design Facebook and Pinterest ads effortlessly." ([Adding keywords for SEO to your Shopify store](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/promoting-marketing/seo/adding-keywords?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

**3. Headings (H1, H2, H3):**
- Use primary keywords in the H1 tag.
- Incorporate secondary keywords in H2 and H3 tags.
- Ensure headings are descriptive and relevant. ([How to Optimize Your Firm's Website for Search](https://www.architecturaldigest.com/story/how-to-optimize-your-firms-website-for-search?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

**4. URL Structure:**
- Create clean URLs with keywords.
- Example: `yourdomain.com/shopify-ad-creation-app`

**5. Image Alt Text:**
- Describe images using relevant keywords.
- Example: `alt="Shopify ad creation dashboard"`

**6. Content Body:**
- Naturally integrate primary and secondary keywords throughout the content.
- Avoid keyword stuffing; maintain readability. ([How to Create & Optimize Landing Pages for SEO (Step-by-Step Guide)](https://ahrefs.com/blog/landing-page-seo/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

**7. Internal Linking:**
- Link to related pages or blog posts using anchor text with relevant keywords.
- Example: "Learn more about our [Shopify marketing automation](yourdomain.com/shopify-marketing-automation) features."

**8. Blog Posts:**
- Develop blog content targeting long-tail keywords.
- Example titles:
  - "How to Create Effective Facebook Ads for Your Shopify Store"
  - "Top 5 Shopify Ad Creation Tools for 2025" ([Keyword clustering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keyword_clustering?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
